Essential PC Components

Learn about each component before building your perfect PC

CPU (Processor)

The brain of your computer. Handles all processing tasks and determines overall system performance. Choose between AMD Ryzen and Intel Core processors.

Cores & Threads Clock Speed Cache

GPU (Graphics Card)

Renders all visual content and is crucial for gaming, video editing, and AI workloads. NVIDIA RTX and AMD Radeon offer various performance tiers.

VRAM Ray Tracing Performance

RAM (Memory)

Temporary storage for active programs and data. More RAM allows better multitasking and smoother performance. DDR4/DDR5 in various capacities.

Capacity Speed Latency

Storage (SSD/HDD)

Permanent storage for your OS, programs, and files. SSDs offer faster performance, while HDDs provide more capacity per dollar.

Capacity Read/Write Speed Interface

Motherboard

Connects all components together. Determines what CPU, RAM, and expansion cards you can use. Choose based on socket type and features needed.

Socket Type Form Factor Features

PC Case

Houses and protects all components while providing airflow. Available in various sizes from compact ITX to full tower cases.

Size Airflow Aesthetics

Power Supply (PSU)

Converts AC power to DC and powers all components. Choose 80+ certified units with appropriate wattage for your build's needs.

Wattage Efficiency Modularity

CPU Cooler

Keeps your processor at safe temperatures. Choose from air coolers, AIO liquid coolers, or custom loops based on performance needs.

Cooling Type TDP Rating Noise Level

Accessories

Complete your setup with gaming peripherals, monitors, and audio equipment. Essential accessories like keyboards, mice, headphones, and monitors enhance your computing experience.

Peripherals Monitors Audio
